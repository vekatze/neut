Module System

In this chapter, we'll see how Neut organizes names.

Section 4.1 (Basics of Modules) explains the basics of the digest-based modules in Neut.

Section 4.2 (Importing Names) explains how to use names defined in other files. You'll also see how digests are used in name resolution.

Section 4.3 (Publishing Your Code) explains how to distribute your programs. Here, you'll see how Semantic Versioning has practical significance in Neut.
